Welcome to Fishing for Puns: Good Eats by the Sea! Established in 2018, we are a restaurant dedicated to providing you with top quality food that is both culinarily and humorously refreshing. The names of our dishes are carefully handcrafted to fit those of our customers, just like the dishes themselves. Whether you find puns amazingly clever or utterly repulsive, you are sure to enjoy your time with us.
